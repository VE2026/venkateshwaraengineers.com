What We Make

Our Product Range

Engineered for food grains, fertilizers, chemicals, and bulk powders — our machines are built for continuous industrial duty.

Product 01
FRK Automatic Blending Machine

Load cell & PLC based weighing system for accurate blending ratios. Ideal for rice fortification and multi-ingredient blending.

  • Accurate mixing with load cell precision
  • 7" HMI touch screen control panel
  • Data storage option available in PLC
  • Suitable for FRK & food grain blending
Watch Video
Product 02
Duplex Bagging System

Gravity-feed three-speed electronic net weighing system with two weighers and a common bag holder — high throughput bagging for bulk materials.

  • Capacity: Up to 120 MT per hour
  • Weighment size: 5–50 kg
  • Accuracy ±10 g (≤10 kg) / ±20 g (25–50 kg)
  • Handles food grains, fertilizers, flour, grouts
Watch Video
Product 03
Air Packer

Uses low-pressure air to fill valve-type bags with free-flowing powders and granules. Ideal for carbon black, soya powder, cocoa, proteins.

  • Capacity: Up to 10 MT per hour
  • Weighment size: Up to 25 kg
  • Accuracy ±0.5% at 2 sigma
  • Suitable for fine powders and pellets
Watch Video
Product 04
Hydraulic Stackers

Mobile hydraulic stackers with motorised power pack — ideal for warehousing, bag stacking, and adjustable-height discharge.

  • C/C distance: 12.5 m (41 ft) long
  • Width: 600 mm | Discharge ht: 7–26 ft
  • 2 pneumatic + 2 solid revolving wheels
  • Reversible conveyor with motorised hydraulic
Watch Video
Product 05
Heavy Duty Bag Closing System

Wooden slat or belt conveyor with integrated stitching machine for two-row, four-thread bag closing. Optional taping and lubrication.

  • Two-row, four-thread stitching machine
  • Optional taping attachment
  • Optional lubrication pump & auto cutter
  • Slat or belt conveyor options
Watch Video
Product 06
Big Bag Filling System

Jumbo bag (FIBC) filling in gross or net mode depending on product characteristics. Available with PLC-based weighing and data logging.

  • Gross mode or net mode filling
  • PLC-based weighing solutions
  • Data logging & tank weighing systems
  • Multiple product dosing available

What We Offer

End-to-End Capabilities

From weighing to palletising — we engineer, manufacture, install and service complete packaging lines.

Weighing Systems
Load cell, PLC-based net and gross weighing, wagon loading, tank weighing and multi-product dosing.
Bagging & Packing
Open-mouth, valve bag, jumbo bag and duplex bagging for powders, granules, food grains and chemicals.
Material Handling
Belt conveyors, slat conveyors, bag transfer systems, 90° bag turners, and bag in-feed systems.
Stacking & Loading
Hydraulic loaders and stackers with adjustable discharge height for warehousing and logistics operations.
Automatic Packaging Lines
Full lines with Technipes Italy: bag placing, filling, check weigher, metal detector, inkjet printer, palletiser, stretch wrap.
Bag Closing
Heavy duty stitching machines with taping, lubrication and auto-cutter options for all bag types.
Blending Systems
FRK and multi-ingredient blending with accurate ratio control, HMI touchscreen and data recording.
After-Sales Service
Spares supply, on-site servicing, AMC contracts and operator training across our installed base in India.
